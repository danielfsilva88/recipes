Ingredients:
 - 300g ground beef
 - 2 cloves garlic, chopped
 - half sliced onion
 - 2 spoon olive oil
 - 2 teaspoon salt
 - 1 teaspoon green onion

Directions:
1. Brown garlic and onion with olive oil.
2. Add all ground beef and twisted it to wishful point.
3. Add salt and green onion and simmer for a few minutes.
